ASTRE Launch

OBC Team Member

The ASTRE Launch project is designed to prepare students for the C-Space initiative, which allows youth to create and launch their own space projects.
As a member of the OBC (On Board Computer) team, I play a critical role in the development and integration of onboard computer systems for our rocket. My responsibilities include:

  • System Design: Creating a robust architecture for data collection and system monitoring during flight.
  • Software Development: Programming in C and Python to manage sensor data, control systems, and implement safety protocols.
  • Testing and Validation: Conducting rigorous tests to ensure reliability and functionality through simulations and test launches.
  • Collaboration: Working closely with other teams, such as propulsion and structure, for seamless integration of all rocket components.
  • Problem-Solving: Applying creative solutions to technical challenges, enhancing my project management and teamwork skills.
